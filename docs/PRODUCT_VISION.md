# Product Vision

AIR exists to reduce the time between test execution and release decision.

Traditional automation reports answer: which tests passed or failed?

AIR answers:

- Can we release?
- Why is that the decision?
- Which business journeys are healthy?
- Which modules need attention?
- What evidence proves it?
- What should QA or engineering do next?

## Mission

Turn automation execution data into quality intelligence and release confidence.

## Product Positioning

AIR is not a Playwright report. Playwright is one input source. AIR is a framework-independent Quality Intelligence Platform that can eventually ingest Cypress, Selenium, Robot Framework, Appium, Postman, API, database, performance, security, accessibility, and visual validation results.

## Primary Users

- QA leads who need release confidence.
- Automation engineers who need fast investigation.
- Developers who need failure evidence.
- Engineering managers who need quality trends.
- Product stakeholders who need plain-language release status.

## Product Principles

1. Solve a real user problem.
2. Reduce investigation time.
3. Avoid duplicated information.
4. Stay reusable across projects.
5. Make purpose obvious to managers, QA engineers, and developers.

## Information Flow

AIR must guide users through:

1. Decision
2. Reason
3. Evidence
4. Action

Every page and feature should support that path.
